## 1.0 Purpose
Define boundary protection, secure communications, and transmission protection requirements for Rivermark Operations Portal (ROP) (Fictional).

## 2.0 Scope
Applies to network paths, APIs, administrative channels, service-to-service communications, and data transmissions associated with Rivermark Operations Portal (ROP) (Fictional).

## 3.0 Roles and Responsibilities
- Security Architecture Lead
- Network Engineering
- Platform Engineering
- System Administrators

## 4.0 Standard Statements
- External and internal communications shall be protected using approved protocols and configurations.
- Administrative access paths shall be restricted and protected.
- Network boundaries and trust zones shall be defined and controlled.
- Publicly exposed services shall be reviewed for least functionality and secure configuration.
- *Intentional gap:* approved cipher and protocol version baseline is not explicitly listed.

## 5.0 Procedures
1. Document trusted interfaces, ports, protocols, and data flows.
2. Protect data in transit using approved mechanisms.
3. Restrict administrative interfaces to approved paths and roles.
4. Review exposed services and ingress rules on a periodic basis.
5. Investigate unauthorized connection paths or insecure protocol use.
6. *Intentional gap:* no explicit frequency is defined for network rule recertification.

## 6.0 Exceptions
Exceptions require documented approval and POA&M tracking.

## 7.0 Review Cadence
Review annually or upon major change.
