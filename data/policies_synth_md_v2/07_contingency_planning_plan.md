## 1.0 Purpose
Define contingency planning, backup, recovery, and continuity requirements for Rivermark Operations Portal (ROP) (Fictional).

## 2.0 Scope
Applies to systems, services, supporting infrastructure, backups, and recovery processes required to restore Rivermark Operations Portal (ROP) (Fictional).

## 3.0 Roles and Responsibilities
- Service Continuity Manager
- Platform Engineering
- Database Administrator
- System Owner

## 4.0 Plan Statements
- Backup and recovery capabilities shall support restoration of critical services and data.
- Recovery procedures shall be documented for critical system components.
- Contingency roles and communications paths shall be defined.
- Contingency planning shall be exercised periodically.
- *Intentional gap:* recovery time objective (RTO) and recovery point objective (RPO) values are not defined.

## 5.0 Procedures
1. Identify critical services, components, and dependencies.
2. Maintain backup procedures for application data and supporting configurations.
3. Store backup evidence and restoration logs.
4. Execute restoration testing on a periodic basis.
5. Update contingency documentation following major system changes.
6. Conduct lessons-learned reviews after exercises or real recovery events.
7. *Intentional gap:* alternate processing site activation criteria are not defined.

## 6.0 Communications
Escalation contacts and continuity communications lists shall be maintained in a controlled repository.

## 7.0 Exceptions
Exceptions require documented approval and POA&M tracking.

## 8.0 Review Cadence
Review annually or upon major change.
