## 1.0 Purpose
Define how risk assessments are prepared, conducted, documented, and tracked for Rivermark Operations Portal (ROP) (Fictional).

## 2.0 Scope
Applies to system changes, identified threats, vulnerabilities, inherited services, and periodic assessment activities affecting Rivermark Operations Portal (ROP) (Fictional).

## 3.0 Roles and Responsibilities
- Risk Manager
- System Owner
- Security Assessor
- Engineering Leads

## 4.0 Procedure Statements
- Risk assessments shall evaluate threats, vulnerabilities, likelihood, impact, and response options.
- Significant changes shall trigger a review of risk posture.
- Assessment outputs shall be documented and tracked to closure or acceptance.
- Risk information shall inform prioritization of remediation and monitoring activities.
- *Intentional gap:* formal scoring methodology is not defined.

## 5.0 Procedures
1. Define the assessment scope and assumptions.
2. Identify assets, dependencies, and trust relationships.
3. Identify relevant threat events and vulnerabilities.
4. Analyze likelihood and impact using the approved assessment approach.
5. Document risks, mitigations, owners, and due dates.
6. Escalate risks exceeding organizational tolerance.
7. Reassess after major changes or material incidents.
8. *Intentional gap:* reassessment interval for moderate-risk findings is not specified.

## 6.0 Records
- Risk register entries
- Assessment reports
- Accepted risk decisions
- Remediation tracking records

## 7.0 Exceptions
Exceptions require documented approval and POA&M tracking.

## 8.0 Review Cadence
Review annually or upon major change.
