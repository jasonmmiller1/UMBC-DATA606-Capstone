## 1.0 Purpose
Define security awareness and role-based training requirements for personnel supporting Rivermark Operations Portal (ROP) (Fictional).

## 2.0 Scope
Applies to all users, administrators, contractors, and support personnel with access to Rivermark Operations Portal (ROP) (Fictional) or related services.

## 3.0 Roles and Responsibilities
- Security Awareness Program Manager
- Managers
- System Administrators
- Users

## 4.0 Policy Statements
- Personnel shall complete security awareness training prior to receiving system access.
- Personnel with privileged or security-relevant responsibilities shall complete role-based training aligned to assigned duties.
- Refresher training shall be completed at least annually.
- Training completion records shall be retained for audit and assessment purposes.
- *Intentional gap:* phishing exercise frequency is not defined.

## 5.0 Procedures
1. New personnel are enrolled in baseline awareness training during onboarding.
2. Managers identify personnel requiring role-based training.
3. Required training is assigned and tracked through the learning platform.
4. Completion status is reviewed and overdue training is escalated.
5. Access for overdue privileged users may be subject to management review.
6. *Intentional gap:* no defined timeline for remediation of overdue standard-user training.

## 6.0 Exceptions
Exceptions require documented approval and POA&M tracking.

## 7.0 Review Cadence
Review annually or upon major change.
