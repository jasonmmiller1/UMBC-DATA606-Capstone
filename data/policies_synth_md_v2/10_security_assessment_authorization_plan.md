## 1.0 Purpose
Define assessment, evidence collection, remediation tracking, and authorization support activities for Rivermark Operations Portal (ROP) (Fictional).

## 2.0 Scope
Applies to internal assessments, independent assessments, remediation evidence, POA&M tracking, and authorization package support for Rivermark Operations Portal (ROP) (Fictional).

## 3.0 Roles and Responsibilities
- Authorizing Official Representative
- Security Assessor
- ISSM
- System Owner

## 4.0 Plan Statements
- Selected controls shall be assessed using documented procedures and evidence.
- Assessment results shall distinguish implemented, partially implemented, planned, and not applicable conditions where appropriate.
- Deficiencies shall be tracked through POA&M processes to closure or risk acceptance.
- Authorization decisions shall be supported by current evidence.
- *Intentional gap:* target frequency for control reassessment by control volatility is not defined.

## 5.0 Procedures
1. Define the assessment scope and control set.
2. Collect policies, standards, plans, configurations, and technical evidence.
3. Perform interviews, examinations, and tests as applicable.
4. Record findings, observations, and recommendations.
5. Create or update POA&Ms for identified deficiencies.
6. Validate remediation evidence prior to closure.
7. Prepare summary materials for authorization decision support.
8. *Intentional gap:* independence criteria for internal assessors are not fully specified.

## 6.0 Evidence Sources
- Policies and procedures
- Technical configurations
- System screenshots and exports
- Interview notes
- Test outputs

## 7.0 Exceptions
Exceptions require documented approval and POA&M tracking.

## 8.0 Review Cadence
Review annually or upon major change.
