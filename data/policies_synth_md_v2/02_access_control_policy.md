# Access Control Policy — Rivermark Operations Portal (ROP) (Fictional)

> **SYNTHETIC DEMO ARTIFACT — ACADEMIC USE ONLY**

**Document ID:** POLICY_AC  
**Version:** 0.1  
**Effective Date:** 2026-02-11  
**Owner:** Identity and Access Management (IAM) Lead (Fictional Role)  

---

## 1.0 Purpose
Define account management, access provisioning, least privilege, and privileged access requirements for Rivermark Operations Portal (ROP) (Fictional).

## 2.0 Scope
Applies to all users and administrators of Rivermark Operations Portal (ROP) (Fictional) and related authentication/authorization services.

## 3.0 Roles and Responsibilities
- IAM Lead
- System Administrators
- Managers
- Users

## 4.0 Policy Statements
- Access shall be granted based on least privilege.
- Privileged accounts shall be separate from standard user accounts.
- Access requests shall be approved prior to provisioning.
- Accounts shall be disabled upon termination or role change.
- *Intentional gap:* access recertification frequency is not defined.

## 5.0 Procedures
1. User submits an access request with justification.
2. Manager approves the request.
3. Admin provisions access per role.
4. Privileged access requires IAM Lead approval.
5. *Intentional gap:* no periodic recertification procedure defined.

## 6.0 Exceptions
Exceptions require documented approval and POA&M tracking.

## 7.0 Review Cadence
Review annually or upon major change.

## 8.0 Glossary
- **Least Privilege**
- **Privileged Account**
- **Access Recertification**
