## 1.0 Purpose
Define incident response phases, roles, communications, and evidence handling.

## 2.0 Scope
Applies to security incidents involving Rivermark Operations Portal (ROP) (Fictional), associated infrastructure, and connected services.

## 3.0 Roles and Responsibilities
- **IR Lead:** coordinates incident response and decision-making.
- **Security Analyst:** triages alerts and collects evidence.
- **System Admin:** executes containment and remediation actions.
- **Comms Lead:** manages internal stakeholder updates.

## 4.0 Policy Statements
- Incidents shall be triaged, categorized, contained, eradicated, and recovered.
- Evidence shall be preserved for forensic analysis.
- Stakeholders shall be notified based on severity.
- *Intentional gap:* Escalation time objectives are not defined per severity level.

## 5.0 Procedures
1. Detect (alerts, user reports).
2. Triage and categorize.
3. Contain (isolate affected accounts/systems).
4. Eradicate (remove root cause).
5. Recover (restore service and validate).
6. Post-incident review and lessons learned.
7. *Intentional gap:* No formal tabletop exercise schedule is specified.

## 6.0 Exceptions
If containment actions may disrupt operations, System Owner approval is required unless immediate action is necessary.

## 7.0 Review Cadence
Review annually and after major incidents.

## 8.0 Definitions
- **Incident:** violation or imminent threat of violation of security policies.
- **Evidence:** logs, alerts, records supporting investigation.
